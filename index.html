How Elections Work

Six essential phases every citizen should understand

01
Do this first

Voter Registration

Citizens must register before an election using a valid government ID and proof of residence.

02
Before voting

Candidate Nominations

Political parties or independent candidates file nomination papers within a defined window.

03
Public phase

Campaigning Period

Candidates campaign within legally defined periods and spending limits under the MCC.

04
Main event

Voting Day

Bring your EPIC card to vote via EVM. Booths open 7am-6pm. NOTA is always an option.

05
Democratic outcome

Vote Counting & Results

VVPAT slips verify EVM results. Results declared once certified by the Returning Officer.

06
Democracy complete

Swearing In & Transition

Elected officials take office through an official swearing-in ceremony.
